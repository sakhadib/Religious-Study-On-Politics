## How to Deploy:

1. **Enable GitHub Pages:**
   - Go to repository Settings ‚Üí Pages
   - Select "Deploy from a branch" 
   - Choose "main" branch and "/ (root)" folder
   - Save

2. **Access the Site:**
   - Your site will be available at: `https://sakhadib.github.io/Religious-Study-On-Politics/`
